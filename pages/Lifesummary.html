Life Summary

Early Childhood

I was born May 25th, 2000 in a small town in Kansas called Salina. I had lived there for 10 years until moving to Stockton, California where I currently reside. As a young kid, I was wild and quite boisterous. With no sense of conscious I literally did whatever I wanted. My life at this point consisted of playing out side with friends, and playing Halo on my Xbox.

Teenage Years

After moving to California I had develop a little more personality for myself. I become more conscious of my actions and what I say. I kinda became more preserved and introverted. That didn't stop me though, I still made plenty of friends in middle school and I consider 8th grade the most fun year in my schooling.

Highschool was pretty chill. I didn't really do much in my classes. I would just show up and listen to music, hang out with friends, and be a poor student. Most of my time was spent playing video games. League of Legends, Cod, GTA, its all I would do after school. Surprisingly I graduated with a 2.4 GPA and I was headed off to college.

Present Day

Yeah collage didn't last long. I spent a year and a half at community collage and it was not a good time. I was broke, my grades where bad, and all I did was play League of Legends. I decided to drop out and pick up a job at an Amazon warehouse. I actually had a decent time there. I had worked there for a year and met plenty of cool people. I actually took up a leadership position there and I was able to further developt my social skills.

But all good things come to an end and I got tired of working there. I decided to quit and move on to a different job. At this point I was 21 and I didn't have any plans. Work, work, work, that's all I did.

I spent almost two years at my last job, but quit because I got tired of the monotony. Doing the same thing over and over again sucked, I wanted more from my life. During my time there, I heard from a family friend that there was a coding program in Stockton that I could join and pursue a career in. I thought it over for a year and finally decided that I had enough! I wanted to atleast do something different, so I decided to join this program! So here I am at 24, taking this class. Hopefully I can stay commited and not get kicked out!
